About Me

I am a student of the Erasmus Mundus European Masters Program in Language and Communication Technologies, currently residing at Université de Lorraine in Nancy.

I started this blog so I could have a place to write down my notes on various academic and practical subjects in a way that is accessible to others. I hope to clarify my own thoughts by committing them to digital paper and to contribute some little value to the vast body of text that is the Internet. I would be thrilled if some of what I wrote also turned out to be useful or enlightening to someone.
